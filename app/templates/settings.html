SMTP Configuration

Email alerts are sent using SMTP configuration from environment variables:

  • ALERT_EMAIL_SMTP_SERVER
  • ALERT_EMAIL_SMTP_USERNAME
  • ALERT_EMAIL_SMTP_PASSWORD
  • ALERT_EMAIL_SMTP_PORT (default: 587)
  • ALERT_EMAIL_SENDER (default: no-reply@practiceoflife.com)
